Day 1: Arrival in Rome

Arrive at Leonardo da Vinci Airport (FCO) in Rome. Transfer to your hotel in the city center. After settling in, take a leisurely stroll around the neighborhood to get acquainted with the local atmosphere. Enjoy a traditional Italian dinner at a nearby trattoria.
